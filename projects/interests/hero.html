Hero - Choi, Minsik

Choi Min-sik is a famous male actor in Korea. The actor greatly influenced the Korean movie market. The actor professionally explained his job as an actor in an interview and captured the hearts of viewers during his highly reliable interview. The actor's strengths are his eyes and tone of speech.
