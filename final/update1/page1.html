Things to Know Before You Adopt

Adopting a dog is a big decision that comes with love and responsibility. Make sure you're ready before bringing a new dog into your home.

Learn how to prepare your home from ASPCA Dog Care.

  • Choose the Right Breed: Consider your lifestyle, space, and activity level.
  • Prepare Your Home: Secure dangerous items, set up a cozy sleeping spot, and get essential supplies.
  • Time Commitment: Dogs need daily care, attention, and exercise.
