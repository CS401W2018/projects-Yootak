1. Title and Topic

Title: Happy Dog Life Guide

I chose this topic because I have always loved dogs and believe they are truly part of the family. However, caring for a dog comes with responsibilities that many people may not fully understand. With this website, I want to provide helpful tips and essential information for current and future dog owners. My goal is to promote happy and healthy lives for both dogs and their humans.

2. Content Outline (6 Pages)

  • Home Page: Introduction to the site, a welcome message, cute dog image, and navigation links.
  • Page 1 - Before You Adopt: Choosing the right breed, checklist of things to prepare, and home readiness.
  • Page 2 - Daily Dog Care: Walking, grooming, bathing, vet visits, and a sample daily routine.
  • Page 3 - Training Tips: Basic commands (sit, stay, potty training), good habits, and correcting common problems.
  • Page 4 - Breed Comparison (Table): Comparing dog breeds by personality, activity level, shedding, and beginner-friendliness.
  • Page 5 - Share Your Dog Story (Form): A form where visitors can share their favorite memories and dog info.

3. Form and Table Details

Form Inputs (Page 5):

  • Name
  • Email
  • Dog's Name
  • Dog's Breed
  • Favorite Memory with Your Dog (text box)
  • Recommended Dog Product (dropdown or checkboxes)

Table Content (Page 4):

The table will compare different dog breeds based on the following criteria:

  • Breed: Maltese, Shiba Inu, Golden Retriever, Poodle, and Pomeranian.
  • Personality: Gentle, Independent, Friendly, Intelligent, Energetic.
  • Activity Level: Moderate, High, Low.
  • Shedding: Low, Medium, High.
  • Beginner-Friendly: Yes, No.

For the table, it will be 6 columns and 5 rows. There are 6 categories.
