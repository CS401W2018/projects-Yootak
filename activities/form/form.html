The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do? when people put some data, it will go to the action field, where the form data will be processed.

The form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do? Depending on the method, people can hide or show their input.

What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"? 'Post' make it hide, and 'Get' make it to be seen.
