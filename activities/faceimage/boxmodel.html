What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?
:Block elements naturally take up the full width of their container, while inline elements only occupy as much space as their content requires. Block elements do not share their width, even if extra space is available.
What is the margin, border, and padding?
:The margin represents the space outside an element, the border defines its outline, and the padding is the inner spacing between the content and the border.
What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?
:For fonts and text, the pt unit is commonly used, whereas units like px, in, and cm are generally applied for other measurements.
When would you use a figure tag?
:This is utilized to assign media content, such as images or various types of media, along with their respective captions.
